💡 Essential Tips
📱

SIM Card

Buy True Move H or AIS at MBK or 7-Eleven. ฿299 for unlimited data 30 days. Much better than roaming.

🚖

Getting Around

Use Grab for taxis — no scams, fixed price. BTS Skytrain for central Bangkok. Songthaew (red/blue trucks) in Pattaya for ฿10.

💵

Money

Withdraw THB at Bangkok Bank ATMs (lower fees). Carry cash — many places don't take cards. Avoid airport exchange, use city ATMs or exchange booths.

👗

Temple Dress Code

No shorts or sleeveless at Grand Palace & major temples. Carry a light scarf. Sarongs are available for rent at Grand Palace entrance.

🌡️

Weather

Bangkok is hot (30–35°C) year-round. Carry water, sunscreen SPF50+, and a small umbrella. Plan outdoor activities early morning.

🍜

Food Safety

Eat at busy stalls with high turnover. Avoid raw salads. Pad Thai, Mango Sticky Rice, Som Tum, Tom Yum, Green Curry — all must-tries.

🙏

Local Etiquette

Remove shoes before entering temples. Never touch monks. Don't point feet at Buddha images. Say "Khob khun kha/khrap" to thank.

🏥

Emergency Numbers

Tourist Police: 1155. Ambulance: 1669. General Emergency: 191. Bangkok Hospital is top-rated private hospital.

⚠️

Watch Out For

Tuk-tuk scams ("Grand Palace closed today, I take you to gems store"). Grand Palace is open daily. Avoid random street helpers near tourist spots.
